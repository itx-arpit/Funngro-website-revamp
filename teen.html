For Teens — Get Projects, Earn & Build Skills

Funngro helps teens find real company projects that pay and teach. Whether you want part-time teen jobs, student freelancing gigs, or short teen internships, we make the path clear and safe.

Who this is for

  • High school students wanting real experience
  • College-bound teens building their portfolios
  • Young creatives and coders learning on live briefs

Step-by-step flow

  1. Sign up

    Create a free profile, add skills and availability — no resume required. Use a parent/guardian email if needed.

  2. Get projects

    Browse short, vetted company projects or get matched automatically based on your skill tags.

  3. Work, submit & earn

    Deliver the project with mentor feedback and receive payment or a certificate. Repeat to grow your portfolio and skills.

Tips for success

  • Start small — take a 1–2 week micro-project to gain confidence.
  • Communicate clearly with mentors and companies.
  • Include project deliverables in your portfolio and LinkedIn.

Frequently asked (short)

Are projects paid?

Yes — many projects are paid. Payment terms are listed per project.

Do I need experience?

No. Projects are graded by skill level and mentors help guide you.
